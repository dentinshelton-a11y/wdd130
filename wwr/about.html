History

Animas Rapids is a white water rafting company located in Farmington, New Mexico. We offer exciting and adventurous rafting trips on the Animas River, which is known for its thrilling rapids and beautiful scenery. Our experienced guides will ensure that you have a safe and unforgettable experience as you navigate through the rapids and take in the stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have trips that cater to all skill levels. Join us for an unforgettable adventure on the Animas River!
