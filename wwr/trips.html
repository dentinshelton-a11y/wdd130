Our Trips

we offer a variety of trips on the Animas River, catering to all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner looking for a fun and safe experience or an experienced rafter seeking thrilling rapids, we have the perfect trip for you. Join us for an unforgettable adventure on the Animas River!

Book Your Trip Today!

Beginners

Our beginner trips are perfect for those new to white water rafting. Experience the beauty of the Animas River while enjoying gentle rapids and stunning scenery. Our expert guides will ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all skill levels.

Intermediate

For those with some rafting experience, our intermediate trips offer a bit more excitement. Navigate through moderate rapids and take in the breathtaking views of the Animas River. Our guides will provide expert instruction to help you improve your skills while having a blast on the water.

Advanced

For the thrill-seekers and experienced rafters, our advanced trips offer the ultimate white water adventure. Tackle the most challenging rapids on the Animas River and experience the rush of adrenaline as you navigate through the wild waters. Our expert guides will ensure your safety while providing an unforgettable experience for those seeking the ultimate rafting challenge.
