New Mexico

New Mexico is a state located in the southwestern region of the United States. It is known for its diverse culture, rich history, and stunning landscapes. Most of the state is covered by desert and mountains, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. New Mexico is also home to several Native American tribes, as well as a vibrant arts scene and delicious cuisine that features a unique blend of Mexican and Native American flavors.

One of the most popular additions to most food in New Mexico is the Hatch Green Chili peppers. This Green Chili is offered as an addition to anything from hamburgers to Steaks and everything in between. There is even ice cream shops that mix it with vanilla ice cream for a spicy sweet treat.
